Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

What we collect

Nothing. No analytics SDK, no advertising SDK, no crash reporter, no account system, no network requests during normal use.

Data stored on your device

Your class roster (student names you enter) and pick history are stored locally on your device using iOS app storage. None of this leaves your phone. If you uninstall the app, all data is removed by iOS along with it.

Student data

The app is designed to keep student data on the teacher's personal device only. We do not transmit, sync, or store student data anywhere. The app makes no network requests during normal use, by design — this is intended to keep the app simple to use under district student-data policies (FERPA in the U.S., similar laws elsewhere).

Permissions

None requested.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from any user, including students. COPPA-compliant by collecting nothing.

Third-party services

None.
